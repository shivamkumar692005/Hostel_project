VIGNAN HOSTEL

Hostels for Men

There are 14 blocks allotted for Men of capacity varying from 200 to 1000 students in each block. The total capacity for Men’s hostel is about 7500.

Hostels for Women

There are 7 blocks allotted for Women students of capacity varying from 250 to 700 in each block. The total capacity for Women’s hostel is 4000.

International Hostel

International students come in huge numbers to SRM for its outstanding learning environment that fulfills their academic aspirations.

Hostel Rules and Regulations

  • No ragging is allowed under any circumstances.
  • Do not harm or damage hostel infrastructure.
  • Maintain non-violence at all times.
  • Respect fellow hostel mates and staff.
  • Follow the daily curfew and other hostel timings.
  • Keep the hostel premises clean and tidy.
